How to apply for a personal loan?

To apply for a loan with us, you need to be:
- At least 21 years’ old;
- Currently in employment with a regular income;
- Resident in the UK;
- UK bank account holder on a debit card.
Simply click Get Started and provide us with a few details to see if you are eligible for a personal loan. You’ll receive an instant decision without it affecting your credit score. Successful applicants can then complete their application and when approved, will get the money same day*

How much can I borrow?

Loans 2 Go offer personal loans between £250 and £2,000. We do not charge any set up fees, and there are no fees for repaying your loan early. The repayment period is either 18 or 24 months. The interest rate and/or term you are offered is dependent on your individual circumstances.
